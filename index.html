About APP

Introduction:

Body Mass Index Calculator you can calculate your Body Mass Index, Waist-to-Height Ratio, Body Fat Percentage and Calorie Consumption (BMR + PAL) to find your ideal weight based on age and gender,it can also be used to find your healthy weight if you want to lose weight or are on diet.
